About The Book Shelf

A literary haven for readers who appreciate quality, curation, and community.

Founded with a passion for the written word, The Book Shelf brings together a carefully curated collection of fiction, non-fiction, and everything in between. We deliver within the Kathmandu Valley (Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur) and offer membership plans with exclusive perks.

We believe that books have the power to transform lives — and we're here to help you find the next one that speaks to you.
